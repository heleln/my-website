About CARE_FLOW

Innovating home healthcare through smart monitoring and real-time nursing support.

Our Story

Transforming patient care at home across Uganda and beyond.

Bringing Healthcare to Your Doorstep

Founded in 2024, CARE_FLOW was inspired by the need to bridge gaps in home-based healthcare services. Our platform connects nurses with patients efficiently and offers real-time alerts during emergencies, ensuring timely interventions.

Operating from Jinja, Uganda, we witnessed challenges patients faced after hospital discharge. CARE_FLOW now empowers healthcare providers with tracking tools, emergency alerts, and route optimization for quicker response.

With over 300+ active patients across East Africa, CARE_FLOW continues its mission to simplify, digitize, and personalize home healthcare.
