Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions

How do I get started with PlanetPure?
Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account, complete your profile setup, and our team will guide you through the onboarding process. We'll help you configure your waste management settings and train your team on using the platform effectively.
What types of waste can PlanetPure help me manage?
PlanetPure handles all types of waste including organic waste, recyclables (paper, plastic, glass, metal), electronic waste, hazardous waste, and general waste. Our platform provides specific tracking and management tools for each waste category.
How much does PlanetPure cost?
We offer flexible pricing plans starting from UGX 30,000/month for residential users to custom enterprise solutions. Our SMB package is UGX 100,000/month. Contact our sales team for a personalized quote based on your specific needs.
Is my data secure with PlanetPure?
Absolutely! We use enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, regular security audits, and compliance with international data protection standards. Your data is stored securely and never shared with third parties without your consent.
Can I integrate PlanetPure with my existing systems?
Yes! PlanetPure offers API integrations and can connect with your existing ERP, accounting, and management systems. Our technical team will work with you to ensure seamless integration and data synchronization.
What kind of support do you provide?
We provide 24/7 customer support through multiple channels including phone, email, live chat, and in-app support. Our dedicated support team is trained to handle technical issues, account management, and general inquiries.
How do I report a technical issue?
You can report technical issues through our support portal, by calling our support line at +256 78011407, or by using the "Report Issue" feature in our mobile app. We typically respond to urgent issues within 2 hours.
Do you provide training for my team?
Yes! We offer comprehensive training programs including online tutorials, in-person training sessions, and ongoing support. Our training covers platform usage, best practices, and compliance requirements.
